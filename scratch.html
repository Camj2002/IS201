CHESS


How to Play:


  1. First Move
    • Move a Pawn forward
    • Develope a Knight
  2. Mid Game
    • Protect your King
    • Take out your opponents pieces
    • Attempt to force your opponents King into a vulnerable possition
  3. End Game
    • Checkmate your opponents King
    • If there is no clear winner, offer up a draw
